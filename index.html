About

I am an undergraduate student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign double majoring in Astrophysics and Data Science. My research interests lie in instrumentation and experimental data analysis for cosmology. I have worked on data analysis for Cosmic Microwave Background (CMB) science and instrumentation for CMB and Line Intensity Mapping experiments.

Research

My research interests focus on magnifying the faintest signals in the universe. I have been working in the Experimental Cosmology and Particle Astrophysics lab under the guidance of Prof. Jeffrey Filippini for 2.5 years. I completed SURFs at Caltech in 2025 in the Observational Cosmology Group under the mentorship of the late Dr. Kenny Lau and Prof. James Bock and in 2024 in the Exoplanet Technology Lab under the mentorship of Dr. Yinzi Xin, Prof. Dimitri Mawet, and Dr. Nemanja Jovanovic. My research work has mostly focused on instrumentation and analysis for cosmology, and I have also worked in High Contrast Imaging of Exoplanets. Visit the full research page to see projects.
